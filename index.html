Welcome to CHARGE REVAMPED 2026

About Us

CHARGE is the flagship technical event by the Electronics Club, Department of ECE, SNIST.

For 10+ years, we have created a platform where students innovate, collaborate, and present practical solutions through competitions, workshops, and exhibitions.

We host over 7000+ attendees and support students with mentorship, networking, and prizes.
