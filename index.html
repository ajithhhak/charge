Welcome to CHARGE REVAMPED 2026

Organized by The Electronix Club, Department of ECE, Sreenidhi Institute of Science and Technology, CHARGE 2026 is one of the biggest student driven electronics technology events bringing together innovators, engineers, and technology enthusiasts. The event focuses on domains such as Electronics System Design, Embedded Systems, Internet of Things (IoT), PCB Design, Robotics, and Drone Technology. Through competitions, workshops, and project exhibitions, participants get the opportunity to explore real-world engineering challenges and transform creative ideas into impactful solutions. CHARGE aims to create a platform where students collaborate, innovate, and showcase their technical abilities while learning from peers, mentors, and industry experts.

About CHARGE

CHARGE is the flagship technical event organized by The Electronics Club, Department of ECE, Sreenidhi Institute of Science and Technology.

For more than 10+ years, CHARGE has served as a platform where engineering students showcase innovation, creativity, and technical excellence through competitions, project exhibitions, and workshops.

The event focuses on domains like: Embedded Systems Internet of Things (IoT) Robotics PCB Design Electronics System Design

Every year CHARGE attracts 7000+ attendees including students, innovators, and technology enthusiasts making it one of the biggest electronics technical fests at SNIST.
